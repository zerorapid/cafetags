Sonali

4.0

The famous Nilofer Chai lived up to its reputation—their signature Irani chai was absolutely delicious. Paired with the classic bun maska, it made for a truly satisfying experience. While the chai felt a little pricey considering the quantity served, the quality and flavor certainly stood out. The food was equally impressive. We tried the pista baklava and the Belgian chocolate croissants, both of which were delightful. The croissant had a perfectly crisp, flaky exterior with a rich filling that simply melted in the mouth. Their burger was another highlight, served alongside a generous portion of fries. There was also an extensive selection of desserts to choose from. The mango cheesecake, in particular, was light, refreshing, and the perfect way to end the meal. The ambiance added greatly to the overall experience. The café is beautifully designed with several Instagram-worthy corners and aesthetic décor throughout. Outdoor seating is available as well, making it an ideal spot for evening hangouts or late-night snacks. A major plus is that the café remains open until 1 AM. For those looking for a more upscale dining experience, they also have dedicated fine-dining floors. Overall, Nilofer Café offers great food, delicious chai, and a charming atmosphere, making it well worth a visit.
